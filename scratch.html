These are some of my favourite places I've travelled to! Here are photos that I took, along with some specific places to go and things to do nearby.

O'ahu, HI, United States

  1. Laie, Hawai'i Temple
    • Attend the temple
    • Explore the Visitors Center
    • Walk around the beautiful grounds
    • Hike up the hill behind the temple to get a view of all of Laie and the ocean
  2. Laie Point
    • Watch the crashing ocean waves
    • Go fishing
    • Go cliff jumping
  3. Polynesian Cultural Center
    • Visit different Polynesian villages with shows and activities
    • Watch the grand finale night show
    • Eat at different food trucks
    • Shop at gift shops

Banff, AB, Canada

  1. Delicate Arch
    • Hike around the lakes
    • Go canoein in Lake Louise
  2. Town of Banff
    • Visit gift shops
    • Try Canadian foods
    • Eat at different restaurants

Arches National Park, UT, United States

  1. Double Arch, Delicate Arch, & Sand Dune Arch
    • Hike to the different arches
    • Go stargazing
